### Nepotism

- **Nepotism (裙帶關係? 走後門? 靠家裡?)**
    - **名詞：** Nepotism
    - **定義：** Nepotism is the act of granting an advantage, privilege, or position to relatives or friends in an occupation or field.
        - *例句：* 指靠關係得到某項特權、職位、好處...，反正就是走後門啦!!!
